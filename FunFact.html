Fun Space Facts

1. Venus Rotation

A day on Venus is longer than a year on Venus.

2. Jupiter's Size

Jupiter is so big that it could fit all other planets inside it.

3. Mars Moons

Mars has two small moons named Phobos and Deimos.

4. Supernovae

Supernovae release more energy in a few seconds than the Sun in its lifetime.

5. Pluto's Status

Pluto is now classified as a dwarf planet, not a main planet.

6. The Milky Way

Our galaxy, the Milky Way, contains over 100 billion stars.

7. Black Hole Gravity

The gravity of a black hole is so strong that even light cannot escape it.

8. Largest Volcano

Olympus Mons on Mars is the largest volcano in the solar system.

9. Light Speed

Light from the Sun takes about 8 minutes to reach Earth.

10. Water in Space

Water has been found in comets, moons, and planets in our solar system.
